Huiz Pool Resort

The best pool in the Western Visayas

You’re tired. Maybe you’ve had a hard day at work, and you're looking for the best place to relax. That’s why we're here—because relaxation never felt so good until you stepped through Huiz Pool Resort’s gates. So come on down, we’ve got your space for freedom, peace, events, nature, and anything under the sun.

The Pool

Huiz Pool Resort offers a clean, beautiful pool built especially for relaxation and enjoyment. Whether you're with a group or by yourself, you'll appreciate the chance to take a dip in comfort.

Waterfall & Slide

Enjoy the naturally-designed tiered waterfall, or slip down our slide into the pool.

"Kids Only" Section

From infant to 5 years, let your kids swim worry-free in the large child's area.

Handicap-accessible

Wheelchair-friendly access provides anyone the chance to take part in the fun at Huiz Pool Resort.

Covered Seating

Available for rental, Huiz Pool offers several covered large seating areas for parties and picnics.

The Gardens

There is nothing more beautiful than Philippines' own natural environment, and Huiz Resort's gardens feature exactly that—unsurpassed beauty in a lush, well-kept oasis. Perfect for school field trips or when you just want to get away for a while.

Bird Sanctuary

If you've never seen a parrot up close, now's your chance—Huiz Resort offers a unique up-close and personal experience with over 50 kinds of birds.

Catering

Hungry? We offer our world-class catering services to you or your group so you can leave your meal worries to us!

Natural Environment

Featuring over 100 different species of flora from coconut trees to pineapple plants, you can't beat relaxing in your own special jungle.

Accommodation

Come stay in our paradise! We offer 4 private air-conditioned rooms as well as self-catering bungalows and can accommodate up to 30 people—just perfect for your family reunion or company retreat!

Short-term or Long-term

Just need a quick getaway? No problem—we offer daily, weekly, and monthly rates for all our rooms and bungalows.

Free Amenities

Air Conditioning, plenty of parking, cable tv, and free wifi are a handful of the amenities available to you during your stay.

Private & Relaxing

You won't find a more exclusive location in Dumaguete to take some time off. Don't just hope for peace and quiet—expect it.

Events

Huiz Resort is your resort for any event you need. With room for up to 300, we also come equipped with tables and seating, decorations, stage, PA system, commercial kitchen, air-conditioned boardrooms, and free WiFi anywhere on the resort.
